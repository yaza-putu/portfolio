Lempuyang

Visitor queue in real time at Lempuyang Mount in Bali.

  1. Portfolio
  2. Lempuyang

Description

Using this app, visitors, agents, and guides may purchase tickets from the comfort of their homes and obtain real-time information about the best time to visit Lempuyang and avoid long lines.
Tech Stack :

While developing this application, I used Full Stack Golang (JavaScript Native), gorm, html, and echo as the library router.
